About Us

Discover Bioinsights

Discover BioInsights is a digital health company building a clinical decision support (CDS) platform to improve patient diagnosis and the quality of care. Our CDS platform will enable doctors, researchers, and life science and diagnostics companies to:

  • Better diagnose patients
  • Perform population analytics using real-world evidence


Our platform revolutionizes healthcare by:

  • Reducing treatment disparities
  • Lowering costs
  • Improving patient trust in doctors

Products

Evidence Based Diagnosis

Clinical decision support platform that helps general physicians make better decisions by identifying similar patient profiles and providing analytics.

NOTE: It does this by using a repository of de-identified patient data.

Evidence Based Triaging

The Triage Platform helps emergency staff make better decisions about patient care by:

  • Triaging patient
  • Identifying similar patient profiles
  • Providing analytics
NOTE: It does this by using a repository of de-identified patient data.

Evidence Based Analytics

Provides immediate and compliant access to population data and rigorous analytics. Helps government organizations, life science research companies, and business development professionals make informed decisions.

NOTE: It does this by using a repository of de-identified patient data.
